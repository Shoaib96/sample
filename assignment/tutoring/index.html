ABOUT US

In the era of science, technology where we are trying hard to sync with future, to develop the competencies for the competitive world, to triumph the summit, to seek new horizon no one has ever seen, many of us do fail to achieve what we have thought of in the early days of childhood. To live the life we deserve there is a need of education. Education plays the most important role to build the character that society needs. There are two types of education. One should teach us how to make a living, and the other how to live. To make choices in life one does not only need knowledge but the kind of knowledge that helps to gain wisdom from day to day experience.

Let us together rebuild this world that we may share in the days of peace.
